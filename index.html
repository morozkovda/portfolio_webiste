Project Gallery



Reinforcement Learning

One of my recent interests has been focused on developing optimal controllers and policies for stochastic and deterministic systems. These systems are prevalent in various industries and have numerous practical applications, ranging from finance and engineering to robotics and artificial intelligence.

One of my latest projects was designing a Deep Q-Learning Network, which combines the power of deep neural networks with traditional Q-Learning techniques, for Lunar-Lander Gym environment. This approach allows the agent to learn an optimal policy for controlling the spacecraft by approximating the Q-value function using a neural network.

Machine Learning

Throughout my academic and professional journey, I have acquired extensive experience in Machine Learning, a crucial discipline for addressing complex challenges and making data-driven decisions. By developing a diverse range of algorithms—including k-NN for image classification, perceptron for spam email filtering, kernelized SVM for time-series prediction, and deep and convolutional neural networks for MNIST digit recognition, BERT model for downstream prediction task of Acceptability Judgements and KentaiBERT for Immanuel Kant’s speech replication — I have developed a profound understanding of various predictive and prescriptive techniques. This invaluable expertise enables me to tackle a wide array of tasks and contribute meaningfully to the ever-evolving field of Machine Learning.

Financial Forecasting

My professional area is Finance, where I employ and test different quantitative methods for improving a decision-making process. In the past I worked on testing two different approaches for short-term volatility forecasting on real-world data, and compiled a methodology for choosing a model depending on the level of market and asset variance.

I also have experience working with the Black-Scholes model. In the past, I visualized relationships between Greeks and various parameters of the model and developed a Delta-Hedging algorithm to calculate the payoff of an option by simulating a self-financing portfolio. Additionally, I studied the influence of the overall number of portfolio rebalances on the model's estimation error.

Currently, my colleagues and I are working on total cost optimization for advance orders in the energy market using quantile regression and stochastic optimization.
